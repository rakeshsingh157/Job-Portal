Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JobKaro

How do I create a profile on JobKaro?

Creating a profile is simple! Click on "I'm a Job Seeker" button, fill in your basic details, add your skills and experience, and upload your resume. You can also add a portfolio to showcase your work. The entire process takes less than 10 minutes!

Is JobKaro free to use?

Yes! JobKaro is completely free for job seekers. You can create a profile, browse jobs, apply to positions, and message recruiters at no cost. For recruiters, we offer both free and premium plans with additional features.

How does the skill assessment work?

Our AI-powered skill assessment generates custom quizzes based on job requirements. When you apply for a position, you may be asked to complete a brief assessment. Your results are shared with recruiters to help them evaluate your expertise objectively.

How are companies verified on JobKaro?

We take verification seriously. All companies must submit official documentation, which our team reviews manually. We verify business registration, contact information, and company legitimacy to ensure job seekers are protected from fraudulent listings.

Can I message recruiters directly?

Absolutely! JobKaro features a built-in messaging system that allows direct communication between job seekers and recruiters. Once you apply for a position or a recruiter shows interest in your profile, you can start a conversation instantly.

How long does it take to get hired through JobKaro?

The timeline varies based on industry, role, and your qualifications. On average, our users receive their first interview call within 2 weeks and secure a job within 4-6 weeks. Many have found positions even faster with our direct messaging feature!

What makes JobKaro different from other job platforms?

JobKaro combines profile building, job search, portfolio showcasing, skill assessments, and direct messaging in one platform. Our AI-powered matching, verified companies, and focus on authentic connections set us apart from traditional job boards.

Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your profile anytime! Update your skills, add new experiences, upload fresh portfolio pieces, and modify your preferences whenever needed. Keeping your profile updated increases your chances of being discovered by recruiters.
